SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT

The best establishments view solid waste management not just as an environmental responsibility, but positive public relations, and in the long-term, beneficial to the brand and bottom-line. In addition to correct waste disposal, a proper plan should also include recycling programs and effectively reduce the volume of waste. CKP provides full analysis and strategies on waste separation and the specification of eco-friendly equipment in kitchens, bars and laundry which comply with green standards and municipal authorities.

05

LAUNDRY FACILITIES DESIGN

A well-designed laundry is the unsung hero of any hotel. you are responsible to ensure that all laundry services for the hotels guests is done as per the standard policies and is met on time. Shortage of clean linen, towels, uniforms, and slow delivery of guest laundry are the result of poor planning and affect a hotel’s reputation. A properly designed laundry facility ensures guests’ garments are cleaned and returned on time, and staff are always dressed their best.
